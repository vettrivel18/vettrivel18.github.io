These Statements allow you to create programs where decisions are taken based on the expression
specified in the statements
1. ```if``` statement
2. ```if else``` statement
3. ```switch``` statement

## ```If``` statement
```if``` block is executed only when the ```if``` expression is true otherwise, the ```if``` 
block is skipped
